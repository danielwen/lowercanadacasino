About

Founded in 1909, Lower Canada College now has about 750 students. In 1995, it became fully coeducational. The campus is big: it features two soccer fields, three gymnasiums and a new auditorium.

Robotics

The school has offered robotics as an extracurricular activity for 8 years. There is a large dedicated workshop and plenty of equipment and power tools.
